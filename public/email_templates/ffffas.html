Building a Brighter Future: Your Generosity Drives U of T's Vision.

In 2022-23, the University enrolled 97,678 individual students in 86,297 full-time equivalent enrolments (FTEs) of courseload activity, including 65,771 undergraduate FTEs and 20,526 graduate FTEs across all three campuses.

  • "In 2022, U of T granted 21,700 degrees, including 940 healthcare professionals and 800 upskilled workers with master’s degrees in health and medicine".
  • "700+ teachers, social workers, and 1,000+ doctorates. Upon graduation, U of T alumni join 640K+ strong network, setting new standards, making global contributions".
  • "Overall, 89% of U of T graduates from undergraduate programs report being employed full-time in jobs related to their program two years after graduation".

UTF: Embracing global students in an inclusive hub with robust academic support. Thriving in vibrant diversity, they gain quality education and enriching global experiences.

At UTF, we proudly host a diverse community of students from around the globe, each bringing their unique cultures and perspectives. Our school is dedicated to nurturing these students, providing unwavering support to cultivate their skills and turn their dreams into reality.

Through unwavering support, comprehensive resources, and a nurturing atmosphere, we empower our international students to hone their skills, broaden their horizons, and fearlessly chase their dreams.
